# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Our Standards

**Encouraged:**
- Constructive, technical feedback
- Welcoming newcomers and helping them get started
- Focusing on what is best for the project and the community

**Not acceptable:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening a GitHub issue marked `[conduct]` or contacting the maintainers listed in [MAINTAINERS.md](MAINTAINERS.md).

Maintainers will review and respond within 7 days. Confirmed violations result in a warning or permanent ban at maintainer discretion.
